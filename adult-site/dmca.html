PleasureHub respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. In compliance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), we will respond expeditiously to remove any content that infringes upon copyrights when properly notified.

How to Submit a DMCA Notice

If you are a copyright owner and believe your protected work has been made available on our site without authorization, please send a written notification containing the following information to our DMCA agent:

Required Information:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature (as the copyright owner or authorized representative).
  2. Precise identification of the copyrighted work that has been infringed.
  3. Identification of the material that is infringing and needs to be removed, including specific URL(s).
  4. Sufficient contact information (name, address, telephone, email).
  5. A good-faith statement that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  6. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notification is accurate.

Removal Process

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice:

  • We will remove or disable access to the infringing material immediately.
  • We will notify the user who posted the content regarding the removal.
  • In cases of repeat infringement, we will ban the offending user's account.
  • We maintain records of all DMCA notices for 3 years.

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you can submit a counter-notification. The counter-notification must include your signature, identification of the removed material, and a statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed by mistake or misidentification.
